Our Story

We, the Muslim Student Association of UWC have been raising funds to help support students as well as non-students with respect to food, clothes or money. This site is designed to help us increase in collecting of funds as well as advertise the need for funds.

Our Mission

  • To provide money for those in need
  • To provide food for students in need
  • To provide funds to allow MSA to see to needs of Muslim students such as Jummuah and Prayer room maintenance
